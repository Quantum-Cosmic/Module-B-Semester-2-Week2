# NSCG Data Analysis: STEM Workforce Trends

## üìã Project Overview

This project analyzes data from the **National Survey of College Graduates (NSCG)** to explore relationships between field of study, occupation, earnings, and demographic characteristics of college-educated individuals in the United States.

The NSCG is a unique source for examining the relationship of degree field and occupation in addition to other characteristics of college-educated individuals, including work activities, salary, and demographic information.
